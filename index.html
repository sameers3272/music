Welcome to EKAM STUDIO’S

Welcome to
EKAM STUDIO’S

EKAM STUDIO”S Is a one stop shop for recording quality audio for anything from recording voice and musical albums, performance videos, podcasts, commercial voice over, ADR, or any other type of project.

WE PROVIDE services like recording , mixing , mastering , beat making and song writing , live streams and podcast , commercial production , dubbing house , voice-over , remote recording.

Recording studios

RECORDING STUDIO

The place where we record our artist’s or bands using mics like AKG’s , SHURE , SAMSON , NEUMANN , etc . An highly equipped room with proper sound acoustics to get industry standard results.

MIXING AND MASTERING STUDIO

The place where all tracks are mixed using pro tools first , Reaper , fl studios and mix mastered using plugins like isotope , ozone , Antares , fab filter etc. a perfect place for an artist and audio engineer to nurture and grow their product for the market.

Control Room

Control room ensures each and every recording is managed properly. It contains all our latest advanced equipments with a power conditioner to ensure smooth functioning of our studio.
